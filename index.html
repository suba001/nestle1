    Our brands

    Nestlé S.A. (/ˈnɛsleɪ, -li, -əl/; French: [nɛsle]) is a Swiss multinational food and drink processing conglomerate corporation headquartered in Vevey, Vaud, Switzerland. It is the largest food company in the world, measured by revenue and other metrics, since 2014.
